Tanky the game



Destroy all the orange cubes!

Don't dare to touch the blue ones, they're friends.

Ignore the grey cubes, they're just grey.




W,A,S,D to move.

Move the mouse: aim.

Left click: shoot.

Hold right click: move the camera.

Keys 1 to 8: load scenes 1 to 8. (Totallly not cheating).
